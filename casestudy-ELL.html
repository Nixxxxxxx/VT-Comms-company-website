Eastern Long Lions Sports Club

VT Comms was appointed by Eastern Sport Club (ELL) owner Neking Group to strengthen its marketing and PR. VT Comms helped to develop media & communication strategies to ensure consistent message delivery and also helped arrange various media activities and beyond the sports page. VT comms has also produced a new promotional video for Eastern Long Lions Sports Club as part of the new PR strategies.

Throughout the years, ELL’s coverage was largely limited to the sports page. With our new strategies on media relations and brand-building, Eastern Sports Club’s exposure in business, CSR, Parenting and Feature pages has increased significantly. By strengthening the club image, ELL achieved recorded 100% full house in the home games of Hong Kong Eastern Football Team in the AFC Champions League. Over 5,000 news reports on Eastern Football Team Basketball Team. Over 10 brands to sponsor Hong Kong Eastern, including Chow Tai Fook, Green Cocacola, Gatorade, Tony & Guy, Eco-lifestyle Fitness, SMI, Crocodile, ANS Nutrition & Sports, Watsons Water, Adidas and Nike.
